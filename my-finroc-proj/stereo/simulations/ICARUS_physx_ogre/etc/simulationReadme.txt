To be able to start the simulation some requirements have to be fulfilled:  

Navigation in the scene:
- switch camera (world_camera/robot_camera): +
- move camera 
      forward: 		w
      backward: 	s
      left:		a
      right:		d
      up:		e
      down:		c
- rotate camera
      yaw left:		numpad_7
      yaw right:	numpad_9
      pitch up:		numpad_2
      pitch down:	numpad_8
      roll left:	numpad_4
      roll right:	numpad_6

- speed-up camera movement and rotation by additionally holding left_shift
